Welcome to KittenLand

Maybe you've already adopted a lovely kitten, or maybe you want to...
Here is the place to learn some ways to take care of him or her (I don't like using "it" to speak about them)

Cats are not as independant as we think... They can be if you make them be used to, but in my opinion, they prefer being linked with people and animals they live with. It mostly depends on how YOU educate and interact with them... on what you want to share with your "baby", beacause you will represent his mother (you will feed him and bring him security, confidence and tenderness)
You can teach your kitten to come when your call him by his name and follow you when you go for a walk
There are several breeds and you can visit "breeds" to discover different skin they can have.
As his mother, you should pay attention to his food. Mosty if he or she is sterilized or/and stay inside a house most of the time. Visit "How to feed my kitten" to learn more about it
